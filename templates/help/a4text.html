For this assignment, you need to research published reaction in print, and possibly other media, to the book you have chosen. Your reception history will be divided into two sections: contemporary and subsequent. "Contemporary" means within five years of the book's publication; subsequent means more than five years after the publication of the book. Once you have written your reception history, cut and paste it into the text area below.
